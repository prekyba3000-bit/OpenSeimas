# Algimantas Radvila

## Summary
- **Name**: Algimantas Radvila
- **Date of Birth**: July 7, 1966
- **Place of Birth**: Šuoliai, Lithuania
- **Political Party**: Lithuanian Social Democratic Party (LSDP)
- **Position**: Member of the Seimas (2024-2028), Vice Mayor of Kaišiadorys District Municipality (2023-2024)

## Education
- **University**: Kazimiero Simonavičiaus University (2018) - Bachelor of Law
- **College**: Kauno kolegija (2020) - Bachelor of Agricultural and Craft Production

## Professional Background
- **1994-2022**: General Director of UAB Alivdara
- **1997-2022**: General Director of UAB Aradva
- **2023-2024**: Vice Mayor of Kaišiadorys District Municipality
- **2024-2028**: Member of the Seimas, elected from the Kaišiadorys-Elektrėnai (No. 59) constituency

## Additional Roles
- President of the Kaišiadorys Small and Medium Business Association
- Chairman of the Afganistan War Veterans Association Kaišiadorys Branch
- Member of the Kaišiadorys District Municipality Council
- Chairman of the Anti-Corruption Commission of the Kaišiadorys District Municipality
- Member of the Budget, Finance, and Investment Committee
- Member of the Control Committee

## Personal Interests
- Sports (basketball, football, swimming, chess)
- Hunting, gardening
