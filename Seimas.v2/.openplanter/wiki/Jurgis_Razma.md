# Jurgis Razma

## Summary
- **Name**: Jurgis Razma
- **Date of Birth**: 14 March 1958
- **Place of Birth**: Žvirblaičiai, Plungė District, Lithuania
- **Political Party**: Homeland Union (Lietuvos valstiečių ir krikščionys demokratų sąjunga)
- **Position**: First Deputy Speaker of the Seimas (Incumbent)

## Education
- **Primary School**: Attended primary school in Plungė district (1965)
- **Secondary School**: Graduated from Plungė 4th Secondary School (1976)
- **University**: Graduated from Vilnius State University Faculty of Physics (1981)

## Professional Background
- **1981-1991**: Senior Engineer at the Faculty of Physics of Vilnius University
- **1991-1992**: Advisor to the Prime Minister of Lithuania
- **1992-1993**: Assistant to Member of the Seimas Tautvydas Lideikis
- **1993-1995**: Executive Secretary of the Sąjūdis
- **1995-1996**: Member of the Vilnius City Municipality Council and Board

## Political Career
- **1996-Present**: Member of the Seimas
- **1996-2000**: First term as Member of the Seimas
- **2000-2004**: Second term as Member of the Seimas
- **2012-2016**: Member of the Seimas, served on various committees and commissions
- **2016-2020**: Member of the Seimas, First Deputy Speaker of the Seimas (since 13 November 2020)
