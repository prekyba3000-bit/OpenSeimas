# Žygimantas Pavilionis

## Biographical Information

- **Name**: Žygimantas Pavilionis
- **Birth Date**: 1971-07-22
- **Birth Place**: Vilnius, Lithuanian SSR, Soviet Union

## Education

- **Bachelor's in International Relations and Political Science**: Vilnius University Institute of International Relations and Political Science (1994)
- **Master's Degree**: Vilnius University's Faculty of Philosophy (1995)
- **PhD in Political Science**

## Career

### Diplomatic Service

- **3rd Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Lithuania for Western Europe**: 1993-1999
- **Assistant of Director of the Political Department**: 1993-1999
- **1st Secretary of the Political Cooperation Division of the European Integration Department**: 1993-1999
- **Head of the Political Cooperation Division of the European Integration Department**: 1993-1999
- **Ambassador of the Republic of Lithuania to the United States of America and Mexico**: 2010-2015

### Member of the Seimas

- **Member of the Seimas**: 2016-present
- **Deputy Speaker of the Seimas**: 2024
- **Chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee**: 2020-present
- **Chairman of the Lithuanian-Ukrainian Parliamentary Assembly**: 2022-present

### Other Positions

- **International Secretary of Homeland Union (Lithuanian Christian Democrats)**
- **Co-founder of United for Ukraine (U4U) global parliamentary network**: 2022

## Political Affiliation

- **Homeland Union (Lithuanian Christian Democrats)**

## Personal Details

- **Married to Lina Pavilionienė**: 1995-2019
- **Married to Asta Mikutavičytė-Pavilionienė**: 2021
- **Has 4 children**
