# NGI Zero Alignment: Skaidrus Seimas

## 1. Relevance & Impact (40%)
### Strategic Fit
Skaidrus Seimas directly contributes to the **"public nature of the internet"** and **"digital sovereignty"** by:
- Creating a transparent, open-source infrastructure for monitoring parliamentary activity.
- Reducing reliance on proprietary or opaque government data portals.
- Providing "Internet Commons" (data ingestion logic and identity linking) that can be reused by other democratic institutions.

### Breakthrough Potential
The project solves a specific **"market failure"**: the lack of high-quality, normalized, and linked parliamentary data. By automating the ingestion from Seimas, VRK, and VMI, we create a "full-stack" transparency solution.
